# 🔬 Research Experience

- *2025.04 – 2026.02*, **Knowledge Graph-Enhanced QA Benchmark for SEC Filings and RAG System**  
  *Advisor: Assistant Professor Rex Ying, Yale University*  
  Developed Fin-RATE, a realistic SEC filing benchmark with 7,500 QA instances built from 15,311 chunks and 2,472 filings. Evaluated 17 LLMs under ground-truth and RAG settings. Contributed to NumCache, achieving 4× context compression while retaining 92% of full-context accuracy.

- *2025.04 – 2025.06*, **Gradient-free Node Injection Attack for GNN via Reinforcement Learning**  
  *Advisor: Associate Professor Dawei Cheng, Tongji University*  
  Led team investigating adversarial attacks on GNNs. Raised accuracy on discrete data from 36% to 95% via a softened embedding mechanism. Designed a dynamic annealing strategy and led manuscript preparation.

- *2024.10 – 2025.05*, **Prompt Engineering in Segment Anything Model (SAM)**  
  *Advisor: Assistant Professor Peter Zhang, Carnegie Mellon University*  
  Proposed a hierarchical taxonomy of prompt engineering strategies for SAM. Analyzed evolution from manual geometric inputs to automated approaches. Identified key challenges and future research directions.

- *2024.09 – 2024.12*, **Generative Dynamic Graph Representation Learning for Conspiracy Spoofing Detection**  
  *Advisor: Associate Professor Dawei Cheng, Tongji University*  
  Researched spoofing detection in financial transactions using GNNs. Independently designed a pseudo-labeling module and contributed to core sections of the final paper (published at WWW 2025).
